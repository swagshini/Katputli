The Art of Storytelling Through Puppets

Welcome to the enchanting world of Katputli, a centuries-old tradition of string puppetry that originated in the vibrant state of Rajasthan, India. These intricately crafted wooden puppets, dressed in colorful traditional attire, bring to life the rich cultural heritage and folklore of Rajasthan.

Each Katputli puppet is a masterpiece of craftsmanship, meticulously carved and painted by skilled artisans who have inherited this art form through generations. The puppets dance and perform through the skillful manipulation of strings, telling stories that have been passed down through centuries.

From royal courts to village squares, these puppets have entertained and educated audiences, preserving the cultural narratives of Rajasthan. Today, we continue this legacy by bringing these traditional puppets to your home, along with the opportunity to learn and create your own pieces.
