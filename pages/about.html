About Katputli

Katputli puppetry is an ancient form of entertainment that originated in Rajasthan, India. These string puppets are crafted with intricate detail and brought to life through skilled manipulation. The art form has been passed down through generations, preserving the rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan.

History

Dating back over 1000 years, Katputli puppetry has been a source of entertainment and storytelling in Rajasthan.

Cultural Significance

These puppets often depict characters from Indian mythology and folklore, serving as a medium for cultural education.

Artisan Craft

Each puppet is handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional techniques and materials.
