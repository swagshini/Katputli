Master the Art of Puppet Making

01

Design & Planning

Begin your creative journey by sketching your puppet's unique character. Consider movement dynamics and plan your materials carefully for a masterpiece in motion.

02

Material Selection

Select premium materials that bring your vision to life - fine wood for structure, vibrant fabrics for attire, and quality strings for fluid movement.

03

Crafting the Body

Transform raw materials into a dynamic puppet through precise carving and assembly. Create seamless joints that enable graceful, lifelike movements.

04

Adding Details

Infuse your puppet with character through intricate painting and traditional Rajasthani embellishments. Dress it in authentic attire that tells a story.
